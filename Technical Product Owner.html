Technical Product Owner - AI/ML, LLM, Java, Python

We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Technical Product Owner with expertise in architecture, design, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Natural Language Processing (NLP), Java, and Python. The ideal candidate will be responsible for leading the technical vision, strategy, and execution of AI/ML initiatives within our organization, driving innovation and delivering cutting-edge solutions to meet business objectives.

Responsibility

  • Define the technical strategy and roadmap for AI/ML initiatives, aligning with business goals and objectives.
  • Lead the evaluation and adoption of emerging technologies and platforms to drive innovation and competitive advantage.
  • Serve as the primary liaison between business stakeholders, engineering teams, and data scientists, translating business requirements into technical solutions.
  • Define and prioritize product features, user stories, and acceptance criteria in collaboration with cross-functional teams.
  • Manage the product backlog, sprint planning, and release activities, ensuring timely delivery of high-quality solutions.
  • Architect scalable and extensible AI/ML solutions, considering factors such as performance, security, and maintainability.
  • Collaborate with software architects and engineers to design and implement robust, distributed systems leveraging Java and Python technologies.
  • Lead the design, development, and deployment of AI/ML models and algorithms, leveraging libraries and frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn, and spaCy.
  • Conduct experiments, optimize models, and evaluate performance metrics to iteratively improve AI/ML solutions.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field; advanced degree preferred.
  • Proven experience (X+ years) in technical product ownership, architecture, and design, with a focus on AI/ML solutions.
  • Deep expertise in AI/ML technologies, including supervised and unsupervised learning, deep learning, NLP, and computer vision.
  • Strong proficiency in Java and Python programming languages, with experience in building scalable, distributed systems.
  • Solid understanding of software architecture, design patterns, and best practices for software development.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills, analytical thinking, and attention to detail.
  • Agile/Scrum certification and experience working in Agile development environments preferred.
